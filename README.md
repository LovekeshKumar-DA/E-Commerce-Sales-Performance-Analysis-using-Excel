# E-Commerce-Sales-Performance-Analysis-using-Excel
## Project Objectives
The dashboard provides insights into customer demographics, sales performance, order status, and regional sales trends using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, and Slicers.
The objective of this project is to analyze sales performance and help business users make data-driven decisions.

##Tools Used
-Microsoft Excel
-Pivot Tables
-Pivot Charts
-Slicers
-Data Cleaning
-Conditional Formatting
-Dashboard Design

##Business Insights

1. Amazon is the highest performing sales channel.
Amazon contributes approximately 35.5% of total sales, making it the largest sales source among all channels.

2. Women contribute significantly more sales than men.
Women account for nearly 64% of total sales while men contribute around 36%.
This indicates that women are the primary customer segment.

3. Adults are the most valuable customer group.
The Adult age group contributes the highest share of sales for both genders, making them the primary target audience.

4. Order fulfillment performance is excellent.
Approximately 92% of all orders are successfully delivered.
Only a small percentage are cancelled, returned, or refunded.

5. Maharashtra generates the highest sales.
